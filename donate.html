Frequently Asked Questions

Is my donation secure? expand_more

Yes! All donations are processed through secure, encrypted payment gateways. We never store credit card information and comply with all data protection regulations.

Can I get a tax receipt? expand_more

Yes! Tax-deductible receipts are automatically sent to your email after donation. We're registered as a non-profit organization in multiple countries.

How is my money used? expand_more

85% of donations go directly to programs, 10% to operations, and 5% to fundraising. Detailed financial reports are published annually for full transparency.

Can I cancel a recurring donation? expand_more

Absolutely! You can cancel your recurring donation anytime through your donor dashboard or by contacting us. No questions asked.
